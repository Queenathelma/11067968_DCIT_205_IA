ABOUT US

Our Mission

At the heart of our mission is a dedication to fostering a dynamic learning environment that nurtures creativity, critical thinking, and technical excellence in the field of computer science...

What We Offer

Study of Computer Science

The study of computer science is crucial in the Information Technology age. Our courses delve into the study and development of applications that drive everyday use, software systems, and ethical means for their reliable and correct functioning.

Programming Skills

At the core of Computer Science lies proficient programming. Our curriculum ensures students become adept programmers with knowledge of modern techniques and tools, applying sound engineering principles to program development and maintenance.

Diverse Employment Opportunities

Our range of courses and acquired skills enable graduates to pursue employment in various sectors of the economy. Graduates are prepared for the computing industry, equipped to contribute specialized skills to project or research teams.

Platform for Further Study and Research

Our department offers a platform for further study and research in established and emerging technologies, active fields of Computer Science research, and exposure to advanced methods.

Degree Programs

We offer two undergraduate degree programs: Bachelor of Science in Computer Science - Single-Major (3:2:1:1) and Major-Minor (3:2:2:1), along with Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. These programs prepare students for a wide array of opportunities in the technological landscape.

Our Vision

We aspire to be a leading hub for computer science education and research in the region, producing graduates who are not only adept in their technical abilities but also possess the adaptability and creativity needed to thrive in a globalized world.
